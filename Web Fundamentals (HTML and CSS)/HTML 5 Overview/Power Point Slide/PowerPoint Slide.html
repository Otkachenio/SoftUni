Heading and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> - <h6>

        <h1>Heading 1</h1>

        <h1>Sub heading 2</h1>

        <h1>Sub heading 3</h1>

  • Paragraph Tags: <p>

        <p>This is my first paragraph</p>

        <p>This is my second paragraph</p>

  • Sections: <div> and <span>

        <div style="background: skyblue;">
            This is a div
        </div>
